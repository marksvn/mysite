Problem

For Business

Restaurant owners often face challenges with an unorganized booking system that relies on customer phone calls. This can lead to confusion among staff members who struggle to determine table availability and sometimes result in double bookings. Additionally, owners are troubled by outdated menus printed on paper or displayed on their website, which fail to reflect real-time changes such as unavailable dishes or daily specials. To overcome these issues, they may resort to manually updating a board with the latest information, adding an extra burden to their workload.

For Customers

When people go out to eat, their main concerns are typically twofold. First, they worry about whether they will be able to secure a table at their favorite restaurant. Second, they are anxious about whether their beloved dish will be available on the menu. These concerns revolve around ensuring a satisfying dining experience that fulfills their desires and preferences.

Solution

Implementing a comprehensive digital system that integrates online table reservations and real-time menu management addresses both concerns effectively. Customers gain easy access to table availability through a user-friendly website or app, alleviating worries about securing a spot.

Meanwhile, restaurant owners benefit from a centralized platform, eliminating confusion from phone calls and double bookings. Real-time updates on table availability and dynamic menu management keep customers well-informed and reduce the need for manual menu updates. This integration enhances efficiency, communication, and overall satisfaction, benefiting both customers and restaurant owners with a seamless dining experience.

DigiBenders website

Task requirement

The octopus is considered to be a very intelligent animal, and the fact that it has a great number of limbs suggests that it is capable of carrying out a number of different activities at the same time (might be unrealistic for real life octopus but it delivers the visual message) Our objective was to make use of an octopus in a straightforward fashion while simultaneously providing it with a futuristic and technological appearance.

Brand needed colors that conveyed technology and progress in a clear and compelling way. The most obvious solution was to use a gradient of purple and dark blue. Additionally, we were able to create a dark theme for social media, but we retained the flexibility to use a light color theme of the same set wherever necessary.

DigiBenders website

Task requirement

The website was DigiBender's most valuable asset, and any time a company asserts that it can assist customers with their digital requirements, the company's own website must demonstrate that it can do so to the customer's satisfaction. The components of the website have been crafted from scratch. The layout's icons, illustrations, and buttons, as well as its color scheme, were designed with the express purpose of conveying a modern and cutting-edge aesthetic.

The Hero section features an octopus that demonstrates the ability to multitask by switching between different activities while utilizing the tools that showcase the company's skill set. The following sections explain to viewers how we can assist them, and then the services section details the various services that we offer. In addition, the homepage contains sections for the tech stack, the team, the clients, and the blog.
