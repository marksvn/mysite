Web Design

Your website is often the first impression people have of your company. A well-designed website can make a lasting impact, connecting with leads and driving sales. But, above all, it provides visitors with a positive user experience, ensuring easy access and navigation. Make the most of this opportunity to showcase your brand and connect with your audience.

Click below to navigate.

Zoomin

Digital Marketing Agency.

Brand Overview

Zoomin is a brand of digital marketing that assists you in connecting with the people you want to reach through the use of their social media strategy, search engine optimization, and video advertisements that are tailored to target specific audience.

Task requirement

Zoomin required a business website and it was essential that the website not only be simple to read, but also explains services and contain content that adequately described the company's offerings and highlighted the breadth of its portfolio. The website should also contain client's testimonials and blog section.

Web Design

UI layout and icons.

When designing the layout of the website, We made sure to keep their brand's theme colors and fonts as provided. The audience can easily connect with the theme and they can understand the working process thanks to the website's sections that explain it.

The "hero" section explains what it is that they do, and the following two sections explain how they operate and the services that they offer. The website also includes a portfolio that has an intuitive user interface (UI) that makes it simple for PC users as well as mobile users to navigate through the various services and businesses. The Testimonials and Blog sections are what come after the homepage proper.

The user experience (UX) is designed with a lot of care, which enables the website to provide a lot of information without making it difficult to read.

Burguesa

Fast Food Company.

Brand Overview

Burguesa is fast-food chain that specializes in burgers. They offer the best possible quality at the most affordable prices. Their target demographic consists of male and female consumers aged 12 to 60 years old.

Task requirement

The company required a homepage for their website that included a menu, information about the brand, the option to download their mobile application, and a blog section. Because the website serves a very diverse group of customers, the user interface (UI) needs to be straightforward and uncomplicated.

Web Design

UI layout and icons.

Homepage has hero section followed by menu. The hero section of the home page features highlighted items, and the menu, which is the most important component of the website, is located directly below it. This menu has several different food categories that can be browsed through using the scroll buttons.

Customers will be able to view six items at once, but they will also have the option to scroll through the selection if necessary. In addition, a search bar can be found on the menu, and the About Us section features information broken up into bite-sized chunks similar to food samples. Customers who are interested can tap on it to read further information. The rest are sections for the mobile app and blog that are presented in a very organized and straightforward manner.

DigiBenders

Custom Digital Solutions Agency.

Brand Overview

DigiBenders is able to assist clients in the expansion of their businesses by developing and maintaining cutting-edge technological solutions. These solutions are necessary for the client's company to become more profitable or to win the market. DigiBenders is a new age full service digital solutions agency based in New Brunswick, Canada. We have the potential to serve as the organization's pillar for providing technology support.

Task requirement

DigiBenders needs a website, and more importantly, a landing page that is up to date and aesthetically pleasing. This page must be able to persuade visitors that the company is competent in the services it provides. Because a website is the most valuable asset a digital service provider business can have, it is essential that it be in the best possible condition.

Web Design

UI layout, icons and illustrations.

The website was DigiBender's most valuable asset, and any time a company asserts that it can assist customers with their digital requirements, the company's own website must demonstrate that it can do so to the customer's satisfaction. The components of the website have been crafted from scratch. The layout's icons, illustrations, and buttons, as well as its color scheme, were designed with the express purpose of conveying a modern and cutting-edge aesthetic.

The Hero section features an octopus that demonstrates the ability to multitask by switching between different activities while utilizing the tools that showcase the company's skill set. The following sections explain to viewers how we can assist them, and then the services section details the various services that we offer. In addition, the homepage contains sections for the tech stack, the team, the clients, and the blog.

Maryam Beauty

Beauty service provider.

Brand Overview

Maryam Beauty is a top beauty service provider in Montreal, specializing in eyebrow shaping and skin pigmentation services. With experienced and skilled team, state-of-the-art equipment and commitment to using only the highest quality products, they ensure to provide beautiful, polished results every time. They have good social presence with 6000 Instagram followers.

Task requirement

In Montreal, being a city where the beauty and makeup industry is very competitive, this business needed an elegant visual brand identity that can help to build trust and credibility with customers, not only making them more likely to choose the business over competitors but also conveys the business's values and offerings, making it easier for customers to understand what they can expect from the business.

Web Design

UI layout, icons and illustrations.

The website for Maryam Beauty is designed to be simple yet effective in providing enough information about the business and attracting clients. The website showcases previous work, which helps potential clients get a sense of the quality of work they can expect from the business. This is a great way to build trust and credibility with potential clients, and it also allows them to see the range of services offered.

The website also makes it easy for clients to jump to the booking page, which makes it convenient for them to schedule an appointment. This is an important feature as it helps to increase the conversion rate for the website by making it easy for potential clients to take action. The website is optimized for different devices, making it easy for potential clients to access it from their smartphones or tablets, which is important as many people use their mobile devices to browse the internet. The website is user-friendly and easy to navigate, so that users can quickly find the information they are looking for.
